About Me

I'm a full-stack sorcerer fluent in MERN spells, Docker incantations, and DevOps rituals. Whether it's building intelligent bots with Rasa or wrangling databases with Prisma, I know how to speak the language of both humans and machines (although machines tend to listen more). I've deployed LLM-powered apps, automated boring stuff with RPA, and trained models that could probably beat me in chess—thankfully, they can't go on treks.

When I'm not debugging the universe, I like hitting the mountains for a good trek, challenging friends to a game of chess (where I still lose occasionally and blame latency), or getting emotionally attached to cricket matches. The harmonium is my analog escape—an instrument that doesn’t crash unless *I* do.

So whether it's shipping scalable software or figuring out the next chord progression on my harmonium, I love solving problems creatively. If you’re into clean code, mountain air, or conversations about why white pawns never get a promotion, we’ll get along just fine.
